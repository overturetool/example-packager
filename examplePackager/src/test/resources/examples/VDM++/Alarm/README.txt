This is the alam example from the VDM++ book, John Fitzgerald, Peter
Gom Larsen, Paul Mukherjee, Nico Plat and Marcel Verhoef. Validated
Designs fo Object-oriented Systems, Springer, New York. 2005, ISBN
1-85233-881-4. The example is inspied by a subcomponent of a large
alam system developed by IFAD A/S. It is modelling the management of
alams for an industrial plant. The purpose of the model is to clarify
the ules governing the duty roster and calling out of experts to deal
with alams. A comparable model of this example also exists in VDM-SL.
